# Content SEO — Strategy, Keyword Research & Writing Guide

## Content SEO Philosophy (Google's Perspective)
Google's goal: Connect people with the most helpful, trustworthy answer to their question.
Your goal: Be that answer.

Key principle from Google's guidelines: **"Write for people, not search engines."**
Content that genuinely helps people will naturally rank well. Content written to 
manipulate algorithms tends to fail over time.

---

## Keyword Research Framework

### Step 1: Seed Keywords
Start with what your business does:
```
Your service + location
Your service + problem it solves
Your service + target customer
Your service + action (repair, install, fix, find, hire)

Examples for a plumber:
- "plumber Austin"
- "fix leaky pipe"
- "water heater installation"  
- "hire a plumber"
- "emergency plumbing"
```

### Step 2: Expand with GSC Data
```
GSC → Performance → Queries
Look for queries you already rank for (even if position 11-50)
These prove Google understands your topic — double down on them
```

### Step 3: Keyword Classification
Sort all keywords into content types:
```
Commercial Intent (Service pages):
- "plumber Austin TX"
- "water heater repair Austin"
- "emergency plumber near me"

Informational Intent (Blog posts):
- "how much does a plumber cost"
- "how to fix a leaky faucet"
- "signs you need a new water heater"

Navigational Intent (Brand pages):
- "Austin Plumbing Pro reviews"
- "Austin Plumbing Pro contact"

Local Intent (Location pages):
- "plumber Round Rock TX"
- "plumbing services Cedar Park"
```

### Step 4: Keyword Priority Scoring
```
Score each keyword on:
1. Relevance: How relevant to your business? (1-5)
2. Intent match: Do you have a page for this? (1-5)
3. Competition: How hard is it to rank? (5=easy, 1=hard)
4. Volume: How many people search this? (1-5)

Priority = Relevance × Intent × Competition × Volume
Focus on keywords with highest priority score first
```

## Content Types & When to Create Them

### Service Pages (Commercial)
**Create when:** You offer a specific service
**Goal:** Convert visitor to customer
**Template:**
```
H1: [Service Name] in [City] | [Brand]
Intro: What the service is + why they need it (100 words)
H2: What's Included in Our [Service]
  List of everything included
H2: Why Choose [Brand] for [Service]
  Differentiators, credentials, guarantees
H2: How Our [Service] Process Works
  Step-by-step what happens
H2: [Service] Pricing
  If comfortable sharing; or "Call for a free quote"
H2: Frequently Asked Questions
  5-8 real questions with answers
H2: Service Areas
  Cities/neighborhoods you cover
Closing CTA: Call now / Request a quote / Contact us
```

### Blog Posts (Informational)
**Create when:** People search "how to" / "what is" / "why" questions related to your field
**Goal:** Build authority, attract top-of-funnel traffic, internal link to service pages
**Template:**
```
H1: [How to/What is/Why] [Topic]: [Specific Benefit]
Intro: What they'll learn + why it matters (75 words max)
H2: [First main point]
H2: [Second main point]  
H2: [Third main point]
H2: When to Call a Professional (link to service page!)
H2: Frequently Asked Questions
Conclusion: Summary + CTA to contact/learn more
```

### Location Pages (Local)
**Create when:** You serve multiple cities/towns
**Goal:** Rank for "[service] in [city]" searches
**Template:**
```
H1: [Service] in [City, State] | [Brand Name]
Intro: Specifically serving [City] (mention neighborhoods, landmarks)
H2: [Service] Services We Offer in [City]
H2: About Our [City] Service Area
  (Specific details about this location — make it genuine)
H2: Why [City] Residents Choose [Brand]
H2: Recent Work in [City] (if you have photos/case studies)
H2: Testimonials from [City] Customers
H2: Contact Our [City] Team
Schema: LocalBusiness with this location's specific details
```

## Content Quality Checklist

### Before Publishing Any Content:
- [ ] Does this content genuinely help the reader?
- [ ] Is every fact accurate and verifiable?
- [ ] Is it more helpful than what's currently ranking for this term?
- [ ] Does it have a clear purpose (why would someone want to read this)?
- [ ] Is there a clear next step for the reader (CTA)?
- [ ] Has it been proofread for spelling/grammar?
- [ ] Are images included where helpful?
- [ ] Is it mobile-readable (short paragraphs, subheadings)?
- [ ] Does it link to related pages on your site?

### E-E-A-T Signals to Add:
```
Experience:
- "In our 15 years serving Austin..."
- Case studies and real results
- Before/after photos

Expertise:
- Author bio with credentials
- Certifications and licenses mentioned
- Technical details showing deep knowledge

Authoritativeness:
- Link to external authoritative sources
- Industry associations mentioned
- Awards or recognition

Trustworthiness:
- Accurate information (cite sources)
- Clear contact information
- Privacy policy and terms present
- Honest pricing / no hidden fees mentioned
- Response to negative reviews visible
```

## Content Optimization Workflow

### For Existing Content (Refresh):
```
1. Check GSC: What queries is this page getting impressions for?
2. Check position: Is it ranking but not on page 1?
3. Read the page: Does it fully answer the intent behind those queries?
4. Check competitors: What do top-ranking pages have that yours doesn't?
5. Add missing information, update outdated info
6. Improve title and meta description
7. Add FAQ section based on "People Also Ask" in Google
8. Add more internal links
9. Update the "last updated" date
10. Request re-indexing in GSC
```

### For New Content:
```
1. Confirm the keyword has real search demand
2. Identify the search intent (what does the user actually want?)
3. Choose the right content format for that intent
4. Outline the content before writing
5. Write draft covering all key points better than current top results
6. Add unique insights, examples, or data if possible
7. Optimize on-page elements (title, H1, meta, images)
8. Add internal links to and from related pages
9. Add relevant schema markup
10. Publish and submit URL to GSC for indexing
```

## Content Calendar for Local Business

### Month 1: Foundation
```
Week 1: Optimize all existing service pages
Week 2: Create/optimize homepage
Week 3: Create location pages for top 3 service areas
Week 4: Create FAQ page for top questions
```

### Month 2: Authority Building
```
Week 1: Blog post answering #1 customer question
Week 2: Blog post on common problem you solve
Week 3: Blog post comparing DIY vs professional (link to service)
Week 4: Create case study from recent successful job
```

### Month 3: Expansion
```
Week 1: Location pages for remaining service areas
Week 2: Blog post targeting secondary service
Week 3: Blog post on industry news/how it affects customers
Week 4: "How to choose a [your service]" guide
```

### Ongoing (Monthly):
```
- 1-2 new blog posts targeting informational keywords
- Refresh 2-3 existing pages based on GSC data
- Update case studies/testimonials
- Seasonal content (holiday prep, spring maintenance, etc.)
```
